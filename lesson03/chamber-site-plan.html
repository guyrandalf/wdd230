Site Name: Ibadan Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Reason: The name represents the city in which the chamber operates, promoting local business and community development.

Purpose 1: "The Ibadan Chamber of Commerce serves as a hub for local businesses, promoting economic growth through networking events, resources, and advocacy."

Purpose 2: "Our website provides a central platform to showcase the city's attractions, demographics, and events, encouraging tourism and fostering local business development."
